---
title: Agile Craft
rarity: uncommon
type: spell
level: 1
traits:
    - abjuration
---

**Cast** {two-action} somatic, verbal

**Range** touch; **Targets** 1 large vehicle

**Duration** until the next time you make your daily preparations

You ward the vehicle with shimmering magical energy. While under the protection of {title}, the base AC of the vehicle is equal to 12 + twice the level of this spell or its original AC, which ever is higher. The vehicle's Fortitude is equal to the AC minus 5 or its original Fortitude, which ever is higher.

---

**Heightened (3rd)** The base AC is equal to 14 + twice the level of this spell or its original AC.

**Heightened (6th)** The base AC is equal to 16 + twice the level of this spell or its original AC.

**Heightened (9th)** The base AC is equal to 18 + twice the level of this spell or its original AC.
