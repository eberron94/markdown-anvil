---
title: 'Regal Mount'
traits:
    - archetype
archetype: Knight Phantom
level: 10
prereq: Greater Mount
frequency: Once per day.
---

When you Conjure a Phantom Mount, you cast the Phantom Steed spell heightened to 5th level. The steed has a Speed of 60 feet, can walk on water, and ignores areas of natural difficult terrain. It can also air walk but must end its turn on solid ground or fall.
