You overwhelm a foe with shadows and whispers drawn from the realm of dreams, filling their mind with feelings of despair and dread. Attempt an Intimidation check to Demoralize a foe. You gain a +1 status bonus to this check. On a success, the target you Demoralizes takes 1d6 nonlethal mental damage.
