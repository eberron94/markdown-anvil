---
title: 'Telepathic Affinity'
traits:
    - kalashtar
level: 9
prereq: Extended Telepathy
---

You possess a natural talent with your latent telepathic abilities. With training you have learned not just to project your thoughts further, but to sift thought the thoughts of other creatures.

The emanation of Quori Telepathy cantrip increases to 120-foot. Additionally, you can
cast Mind Reading once per hour as an innate occult spell, but you can target any creature in the
emanation of your Quori Telepathy.
