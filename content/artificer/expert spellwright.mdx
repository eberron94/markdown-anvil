---
title: 'Expert Spellwright'
traits:
    - archetype
archetype: Artificer
level: 12
prereq: Artificer Dedication, Spellwright
---

When you create wands for Spellwright, you can create a fourth wand with a 4th level spell.

At 14th level, you can create a fifth wand which contains a 5th level spell. At 16th level, you can create a sixth wand which contains a 6th level spell.

Additionally, when you use wands and scrolls that you have tricked, you are considered an expert for spell attack rolls and spell DCs, using your Intelligence modifier, with the spells it casts.
