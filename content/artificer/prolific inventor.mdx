---
title: 'Prolific Inventor'
traits:
    - archetype
archetype: Artificer
level: 4
prereq: Artificer Dedication, Expert in Crafting
---

You gain the Inventor skill feat, even if you don't meet that feat's prerequisites.

When you take this feat, add the formulas for two common magic items up to 4th level to your formula book. Whenever you take an artificer archetype feat, you gain an additional formula of up to the level that you took the feat at.
