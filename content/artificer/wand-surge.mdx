title: 'Wring Wand'
traits:
    - archetype
archetype: Artificer
level: 8
prereq: Artificer Dedication
---

You have learned how to force every last drop of power out of your wands.

You gain the Wand-Surge focus spell. If you don't already have one, you gain a focus pool of 1 Focus Point, which you can Refocus by tinkering with your magic items.
