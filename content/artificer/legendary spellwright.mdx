---
title: 'Master Spellwright'
traits:
    - archetype
archetype: Artificer
level: 18
prereq: Artificer Dedication, Expert Spellwright
---

When you create wands for Spellwright, you can create a seventh wand with a 7th level spell.

At 20th level, you can create a eighth wand which contains a 8th level spell.

Additionally, when you use wands and scrolls that you have tricked, you are considered a master for spell attack rolls and spell DCs, using your Intelligence modifier, with the spells it casts.
