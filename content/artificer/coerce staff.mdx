---
title: 'Coerce Staff'
traits:
    - archetype
    - artificer
archetype: Artificer
level: 4
prereq: Artificer Dedication
---

During your daily preparations, you can prepare a magical staff and infuse it with an Eberron dragonshard. The staff gains 1 charge, or adds 1 charge to its usual amount. You can Cast a Spell from a staff by tricking it like a wand or scroll.

At 7th level and ever two levels after that, the staff gains 1 additional charge, up to a maximum of 5 charges at 15th level.
