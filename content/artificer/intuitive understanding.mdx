archetype: Artificer

prereq: Artificer Dedication, Master in Crafting

Your study of the different magic traditions has created an intuitive understanding of simple magic items.

All 1st and 2nd level magic items that require you to trick them with a skill you are an expert in are always tricked in your hands.
