---
title: 'Warforged Weapon Familiarity'
traits:
    - warforged
level: 1
---

Your wartime training makes you trained with all sorts of weapons. You are trained with the armblade, armbow, and battlefist. In addition, you gain access to all uncommon warforged weapons. For you, martial warforged weapons are simple weapons and advanced warforged weapons are martial weapons.
