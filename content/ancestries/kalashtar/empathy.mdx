You can cast the empathy cantrip as an innate occult spell at will. A cantrip is heightened to a spell level equal to half your level rounded up. Additionally, when you critically fail the check for Make an Impression, you get a failure instead.

**Range** 15 foot; **Target** 1 creature

**Duration** sustained up to 1 minute

You detect the surface emotions of the targeted creature, learning their most basic needs, drives, and emotions. emotions. Thirst, hunger, fear, fatigue, pain, rage, hatred, uncertainly, curiosity, friendliness and many other kinds of sensation and mood can all be perceived.

When you cast this spell, and each time you use an action to Sustain this spell, the GM tells you the targets current mood. As a free action, you can Dismiss this spell to gain a +1 circumstance bonus to a Deception, Diplomacy, Intimidate, or Perception check to Sense Motive against the targeted creature.

**Heightened (5th)** The circumstance bonus increases to +2.

**Amp** The spell gains the emanation trait and targets all creatures in a 15-foot aura. You read the general emotions of all targeted creatures.
