---
title: 'Psi-Blade'
traits:
    - kalashtar
    - concentration
level: 1
action: one-action
---

You can manifest weapons of the mind that cut just as well as a true blade. You manifest a psi-blade in a free hand. These simple weapons deal 1d4 slashing damage. The blades are in the Knife group and have the agile, finesse, magical, and twin traits. You can choose to dissipate the blades at any time as a free action.
