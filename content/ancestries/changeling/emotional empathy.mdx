---
title: 'Emotional Empathy'
traits:
    - changeling
level: 1
---

**Frequency** Once per minute

You are in tune with the emotions of those around you. You gain a +2 circumstance bonus to Perception checks to sense motive. If you succeed on a sense motive check (even if it reveals nothing), you gain a +1 circumstance bonus to the next Diplomacy, Deception, or Intimidation check you make to interact with the creature.
