---
title: 'Terrifying Shift'
traits:
    - changeling
level: 13
---

**Frequency** Once per 10 minutes

With a rapid change of your form, you take on a monstrous form for an instant before shifting back. When you Demoralize, you can choose to replace the auditory trait and with the visual trait. You can then attempt a demoralize check against each enemy that can see you within 30 feet.
