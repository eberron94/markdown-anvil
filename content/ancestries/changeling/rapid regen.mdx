---
title: 'Rapid Regeneration'
traits:
    - changeling
level: 9
action: one-action
prereq: Rapid Shift
---

**Frequency** Once per day

You're able to shift your shape to repair wounds and injuries. You gain fast healing equal to half your level for 1 minute. While Rapid Regeneration is active, you revert to your base changeling form and can't use shift shape. This counts against the frequency of your shift shape.
