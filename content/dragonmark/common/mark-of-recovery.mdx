---
title: 'Mark of Survival'
traits:
    - dragonmark
prereq: 'Lesser Dragonmark'
level: 8
action: reaction
frequency: once per day
---

When an attack would reduce you to 0 Hit Points, you can reduce the damage of the attack by 2d8+5. If you have a Greater Dragonmark or Grand Dragonmark, the damage is instead reduced by 3d8+10 or 4d8+20 respectively.
