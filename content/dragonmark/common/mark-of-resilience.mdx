---
title: 'Mark of Resilience'
traits:
    - dragonmark
prereq: 'Lesser Dragonmark'
level: 8
action: reaction
frequency: once per day
---

When make a saving throw but before you know the results, you can gain a +2 circumstance bonus. If you have a Greater Dragonmark or Grand Dragonmark, the bonus increases to a +3 or +4 respectively.
