---
title: 'Scrying Sense Expert'
traits:
    - archetype
    - dragonmark
    - mark of detection
archetype: Mark of Detection
level: 16
prereq: Mark of Detection Dedication, Scrying Sense Competence
---

When you cast Detect Scrying as a dragonmark spell, you also detect the presence of detection effects in the area. A detection effect is the same as a scrying effect for the purposes of Detect Scrying.
