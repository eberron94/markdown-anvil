title: 'Tear Apart'
traits:
    - archetype
    - dragonmark
    - mark of making
archetype: Mark of Making
level: 6
prereq: Mark of Making Dedication
---

Against construct creatures, you gain a circumstance bonus to damage with weapons and unarmed attacks equal to the number of weapon damage dice for the Strike. This bonus increases to double the number of weapon damage dice if your target has the living construct trait.
