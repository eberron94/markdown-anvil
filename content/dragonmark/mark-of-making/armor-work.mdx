archetype: Mark of Making
level: 4
prereq: Mark of Making Dedication
---

Your mechanical understanding helps you handle complex gear. You gain the Armor Assist feat, even if you don't meet that feat's prerequisites, and can make a Craft check instead of Athletics or Warfare Lore. The Armor Assist feat can be used reduce the time for a warforged to don equipment with the Attachment trait.
