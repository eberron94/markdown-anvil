---
title: 'Cursed Horse'
traits:
    - curse
    - hybrid form
    - beast form
label: form
---

You are cursed by the spirit of horses, or similar equine.

While in Hybrid Form, apply the following:

-   Your Size is Medium.
-   You gain a jaw Strike (a unarmed attack that deals 1d10 piercing damage).
-   You gain a hoof Strike (an agile unarmed attack that deals 1d6 bludgeoning damage).
-   You gain the senses low-light vision, scent (imprecise, 30 feet).

While in Beast Form, apply the following:

-   Your Size is Medium.
-   Your Speed becomes 40 feet.
-   You gain a jaw Strike (a unarmed attack that deals 1d8 piercing damage).
-   You gain a hoof Strike (an agile unarmed attack that deals 1d6 bludgeoning damage).
-   You gain the senses low-light vision, scent (imprecise, 30 feet).
