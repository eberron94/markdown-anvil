---
title: 'Cursed Bat'
traits:
    - curse
    - hybrid form
    - beast form
label: form
---

You are cursed by the spirit of bats.

While in Hybrid Form, apply the following:

-   Your Size is Medium.
-   You gain a jaw Strike (a finesse unarmed attack that deals 1d8 piercing damage).
-   You gain a claw Strike (an agile finesse unarmed attack that deals 1d6 slashing damage).
-   You gain the senses echolocation (precise) 5 feet, low-light vision.

While in Beast Form, apply the following:

-   Your Size is Small.
-   Your Speed becomes 15 feet and fly 30 feet.
-   You gain a jaw Strike (a finesse unarmed attack that deals 1d6 piercing damage).
-   You gain a wing Strike (an agile finesse unarmed attack that deals 1d4 slashing damage).
-   You gain the senses echolocation (precise) 20 feet, low-light vision.
