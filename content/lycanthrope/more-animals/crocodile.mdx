---
title: 'Cursed Crocodile'
traits:
    - curse
label: form
---

You are cursed by the crocodiles or a similar crocodilian reptiles, such as alligators or caimans.

While in Hybrid Form, apply the following:

-   Your Size is Medium.
-   You gain a jaw Strike (a unarmed attack that deals 1d10 piercing damage).
-   You gain a tail Strike (a finesse unarmed attack that deals 1d6 bludgeoning damage).
-   You gain the senses low-light vision, scent (imprecise, 30 feet).

While in Beast Form, apply the following:

-   Your Size is Large.
-   Your Speed becomes 20 feet, swim 25 feet.
-   You gain a jaw Strike (a unarmed attack that deals 1d8 piercing damage).
-   You gain a tail Strike (a finesse unarmed attack that deals 1d6 bludgeoning damage).
-   You gain the senses low-light vision, scent (imprecise, 30 feet).
-   You can hold your breath for 2 hours.
