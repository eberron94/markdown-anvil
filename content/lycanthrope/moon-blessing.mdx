archetype: Lycanthrope
prereq: 'Lycanthrope Dedication'
level: 4
---

When you enter Hybrid Form or Beast Form from your Humanoid Form, you gain a number of temporary Hit Points equal to your level plus your Constitution modifier. When you change to your Humanoid Form, you lose any remaining temporary Hit Points from Moon's Blessing.
