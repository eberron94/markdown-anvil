**Parent Language** Siberian, Nessen

**Script** Siberian

**Frequently Used By** druids

Vvaark is the hybrid language of Nessen and Siberian, which uses the Siberian script. The hybrid formed when the rogue dragon Vvaraak taught the orcs primal magic in order to prevent a prophesied disaster.
