**Parent Language** None

**Script** Frosh

**Frequently Used By** ancient dwarves

Frosh is a green language used by the early dwarves, when they still lived on Frostfell. Its translation is incomplete since Mercantin d'Sivis disappeared in Frostfell shortly after sending his findings back to Khorvaire.
