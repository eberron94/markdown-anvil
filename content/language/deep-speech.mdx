**Parent Language** None

**Script** Unknown (Volaar)

**Frequently Used By** Daelkyr

Deep Speech is a spoken blue language used by the Daelkyr. All of its translations is in Volaar found in ancient Dhakaan writings. As a result, the academic script used for Deep Speech is Volaar, but it is understood that is not what the Daelkyr used.
