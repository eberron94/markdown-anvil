**Parent Language** Siberian, Old Brakia

**Script** Brakia

**Frequently Used By** modern giants

Brakia is the hybrid language of Old Brakia and Siberian, which uses the Brakia script. The hybrid formed when dragons made contact with the giants some 60,000 years ago and taught the giants magic.
