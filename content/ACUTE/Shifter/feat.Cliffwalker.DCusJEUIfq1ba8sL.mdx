You gain the [Combat Climber](https://2e.aonprd.com/Search.aspx?Query=Combat%20Climber) feat as a bonus feat, and as long as you aren't wearing footwear, you can use your strong legs to climb, leaving your hands free. Additionally, if you roll a success on an Athletics check to climb, you get a critical success instead.

While you are shifted:

- You gain a climb Speed of 10 feet.
