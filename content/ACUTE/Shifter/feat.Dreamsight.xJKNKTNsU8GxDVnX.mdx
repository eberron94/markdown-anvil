Choose one cantrip from the primal spell list. You can Cast this Spell as a primal innate spell at will. A cantrip is heightened to a spell level equal to half your level rounded up. You gain the trained proficiency rank in primal spell attack rolls and spell DCs, and your key spellcasting ability is Charisma.

While you are shifted:

- You can speak to animals, as if under the effect of [Speak with Animals](https://2e.aonprd.com/Search.aspx?Query=Speak%20with%20Animals)
