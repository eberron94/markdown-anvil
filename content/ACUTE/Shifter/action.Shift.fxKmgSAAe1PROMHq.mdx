You tap into your inner beast and your appearance becomes more beastial. This shift lasts for 1 minute. You can't voluntarily stop shifting. While you are shifted:

- You apply the shifting abilities from your heritage.

- You can't use actions with the concentrate trait unless they also have the rage trait. You can Seek while shifted.
