Though you typically move like a biped, you can get on all fours to move quickly. If you have both hands free, you can increase your Speed to 30 feet as you run on all fours.

While you are shifted:

- Your horns grow into terrifying weapons, you gain a horn unarmed attack that deals 1d8 piercing damage. Your horns are in the brawling group and have the shove and unarmed traits.
