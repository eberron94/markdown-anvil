---
title: 'Grinding Strike'
traits:
    - dragonmark
    - mark of making
level: 5
prereq: Lesser Dragonmark
---

You know just where to strike a construct to take it down. Your weapons and unarmed attacks gain the deadly d6 weapon trait when you attack a construct. If the weapon or unarmed attack already has the deadly trait, increase the size of the deadly damage die by one step instead of giving it deadly d6.

---

**Greater Dragonmark** The deadly trait increases to a d8.

**Siberys Dragonmark** The deadly trait increases to a d10.
