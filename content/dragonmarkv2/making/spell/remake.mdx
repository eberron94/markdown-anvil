**Cast** 1 hour; dragonmark (siberys), somatic

**Range** 5 feet

**Frequency** You can only cast this spell once per daily preparation - even if you have the focus points to cast it.

With the powers of your mark, you can restore any item to its full grandeur. You can restore an item as if you cast the Remake spell.
