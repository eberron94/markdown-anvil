---
title: 'Blood of the Dauntless'
traits:
    - mark of sentinel
    - dragonmark
level: 3
---

You can shake off effects that would incapacitate you on the battlefield. When you would gain the Stunned condition you reduce its value by 1 (to a minimum of 1). When you have the Confused condition you may spend your actions to do nothing or raise a shield on your turn.
