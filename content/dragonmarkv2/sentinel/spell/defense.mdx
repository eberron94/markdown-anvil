title: Sentinel's Defense
type: spell
label: focus
level: 2
traits:
    - abjuration
    - dragonmark
    - mark of sentinel
---

**Cast** {one-action} dragonmark (least), somatic

**Duration** sustained up to 1 minute

**Range** touch; **Targets** 1 willing living creature

You draw from the power of your dragonmark to better defend a creature. The target gains a +2 status bonus to AC.

---

**Grand Dragonmark** The spell can be sustained up to 1 hour.
