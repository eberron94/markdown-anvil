**Cast** {two-action} dragonmark (least), somatic

**Duration** 1 minute

With a warm handshake and a friendly smile, the powers of your dragonmark help others open themselves to you. The next creature you shake hands with may make a Will save if they are unwilling. They gain a +4 circumstance bonus to this save if you or your allies recently threatened or were hostile to it. Creatures are always unaware that you have cast {title}. The creature is immune to all future uses of {title} from you. The spell ends after your first interaction.

**Success** No change in attitude.

**Failure** The creature's attitude towards you increases by one step.

**Critical Failure** The creature's attitude towards you increases by two steps.

---

**Greater Dragonmark** The spell ends after your second interaction.

**Grand Dragonmark** The spell ends after your third interaction.

**Siberys Dragonmark** The spell does not end after interacting with a creature.
