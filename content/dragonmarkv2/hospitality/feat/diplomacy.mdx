---
title: 'Grace of Ghallanda'
traits:
    - fortune
    - mark of hospitality
    - dragonmark
level: 1
prereq: trained in Diplomacy
action: free-action
---

**Frequency** once per day

**Trigger** you fail a Diplomacy check.

Formal training with house Ghallanda allows you to upkeep your social grace. If you roll a failure on a Diplomacy check, you can reroll the check. You may select the better of the two rolls.
