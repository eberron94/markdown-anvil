**Cast** {one-action} dragonmark (least), somatic

**Range** 30 feet; **Targets** 1 living creature

You call upon your dragonmark's power to heal the wounded. Your dragonmark channels a burst of positive energy, healing a living creature. If you use healing word on a living target, they restore 4 Hit Points.

---

**Heightened (+1)** The amount of healing increases by 4.

---

**Greater Dragonmark** The range increases to 60 feet.

**Grand Dragonmark** The range increases to 90 feet.

**Siberys Dragonmark** The range increases to 120 feet.
