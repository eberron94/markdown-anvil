traits:

    - dragonmark
    - mark of handling

**Cast** {two-action} dragonmark (lesser), somatic

**Range** 30 feet; **Targets** 1 willing creature

**Duration** 1 minute

Grant a willing creature a +1 status bonus to Dexterity-based checks and DCs, and Strength-based rolls and DCs. This includes AC, Reflex saves, ranged attack rolls, melee attack rolls, and skill checks using Acrobatics, Athletics, Stealth, and Thievery.

---

**Grand Dragonmark** You may target yourself and 1 other willing creature.
