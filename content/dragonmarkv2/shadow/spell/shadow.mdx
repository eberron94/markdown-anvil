**Cast** {two-action} dragonmark (least), somatic; **Requirements** You are in dim light or darkness

**Duration** sustained up to 10 minutes.

You blend yourself with shadow, losing visual definition. For the duration, while in dim light or darkness, you gain a +2 status bonus to hide and sneak.
