title: 'Thuranni Spectre'
traits:
    - fortune

level: 1
prereq: trained in Stealth
action: free-action
---

**Frequency** once per day

**Trigger** you fail a Stealth check.

Your training with house Thuranni grants you an understanding of using the light and shadow around you to your advantage. If you roll a failure on a stealth check, you can reroll the check. You may select the better of the two rolls.
