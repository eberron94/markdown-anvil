---
title: Duraak'ash Insight
rarity: uncommon
traits:
    - mark of finding
    - dragonmark
level: 9
req: Lesser Dragonmark and Hunt Prey
access: Must have received training from the Duraak'ash.
---

When you replicate [Locate](https://2e.aonprd.com/Spells.aspx?ID=173) with Lesser Seeker, you can locate a specific creature you have never met.

When you roll initiative and can see the target of your Locate, you can Hunt Prey as a free action, designating that enemy.
