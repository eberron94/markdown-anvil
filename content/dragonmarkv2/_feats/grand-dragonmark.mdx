---
title: 'Grand Dragonmark'
traits:
    - dragonmark
prereq: 'Greater Dragonmark'
level: 13
---

Your dragonmark expands across your body, swelling with power. You gain the Grand dragonmark focus spell appropriate to your mark. Your proficiency in dragonmark attack rolls and DCs increases to Master. You also gain the grand dragonmark trait.

The rules for dragonmark spells are summarized in the Key Terms section on the dragonmark page, and the full rules for focus spells appear [here](https://2e.aonprd.com/Rules.aspx?ID=276).
