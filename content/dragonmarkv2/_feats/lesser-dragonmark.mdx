---
title: 'Lesser Dragonmark'
traits:
    - dragonmark
prereq: 'Least Dragonmark'
level: 5
---

Your dragonmark grows in power. You gain the Lesser dragonmark focus spell appropriate to your mark. Your proficiency in dragonmark attack rolls and DCs increases to Expert. You also gain the lesser dragonmark trait.

The rules for dragonmark spells are summarized in the Key Terms section on the dragonmark page, and the full rules for focus spells appear [here](https://2e.aonprd.com/Rules.aspx?ID=276).
