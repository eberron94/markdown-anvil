---
title: 'Greater Dragonmark'
traits:
    - dragonmark
prereq: 'Lesser Dragonmark'
level: 9
---

Your dragonmark grows in power once again. You gain the Greater dragonmark focus spell appropriate to your mark. Increase the number of Focus Points in your focus pool by 1. You also gain the greater dragonmark trait.

The rules for dragonmark spells are summarized in the Key Terms section on the dragonmark page, and the full rules for focus spells appear [here](https://2e.aonprd.com/Rules.aspx?ID=276).
