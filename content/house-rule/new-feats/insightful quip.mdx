---
title: 'Insightful Quip'
traits:
    - general
    - skill
level: 7
prereq: Wisdom 16; expert in Performance
---

In situations where you have had time to observe the target when you Gibe, you gain a +1 circumstance bonus to your Performance check. If your Wisdom score is 20 or higher and you are a master in Performance, this bonus increases to +2.

When a creature is hostile to you, you gain the circumstance bonus only after you have observed the target failing an attack roll, saving throw, or skill check.
