**Optional Rule** [Ancestry Paragon](https://2e.aonprd.com/Rules.aspx?ID=1336)

At 1st level, you gain two ancestry feats. At every odd level thereafter, you gain another ancestry feats. This is instead of the normal ancestry feat you gain at 5th, 9th, 13th, and 17th.
