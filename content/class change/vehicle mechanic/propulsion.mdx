{title} adds the following propulsion methods to its list.

**Elemental (Air)** The elemental revels in the expression of its nature. Creatures adjacent to your signature vehicle when it begins its movement take electricity damage equal to your level.

**Elemental (Earth)** The elemental pulls harder as its nature is the move the very earth below you. The vehicle gains a +10 circumstance bonus to its Speed.

**Elemental (Fire)** The elemental revels in the expression of its nature. Creatures adjacent to your signature vehicle when it begins its movement take fire damage equal to your level.

**Elemental (Water)** The elemental shifts the waters as easily as a the moons. During this movement, your signature vehicle can turn normally, rather than needing to move in a straight line as normal for a 2- or 3-action Drive.
