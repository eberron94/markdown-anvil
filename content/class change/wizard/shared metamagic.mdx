---
title: 'Meta Sharing'
traits:
    - wizard
level: 4
req: Metamagical Experimentation thesis
---

You gain a one sorcerer feat with the metamagic trait. For the purpose of prerequisites, your sorcerer level is your character level minus 2. Additionally, during your daily preparations, you can choose a metamagic sorcerer feat instead of a metamagic wizard feat for Metamagical Experimentation.

**Special** You can select this feat more than once. Each time you do, you gain another sorcerer feat.
