**Bomber** You can choose Dexterity as your key ability score.

**Chirurgeon** You become trained in Medicine and can attempt a Crafting check instead of a Medicine check for any of Medicine's untrained and trained uses, in addition to any abilities granted by Medicine skill feats (e.g. Battle Medicine). When your Crafting proficiency rank increases with Alchemical Discoveries, your Medicine proficiency rank also increases. When selecting a skill feat for Alchemical Discoveries, you can choose a skill feat for Crafting or Medicine.

**Mutagenist** You can choose Strength as your key ability score.

**Toxicologist** You can choose Dexterity as your key ability score.
