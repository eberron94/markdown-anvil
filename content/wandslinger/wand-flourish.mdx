---
title: 'Wand Flourish'
traits:
    - archetype
    - flourish
archetype: Wandslinger
level: 6
prereq: Wandslinger Dedication
action: free-action
req: Cast a Spell from a wand
---

By flowing the energy from a spell through a wand as a focus, you gain more precise aim, at the cost of damaging the wand. You gain a +1 item bonus to spell attack rolls for that spell. The wand then becomes broken.
