---
title: 'Wandcrafter'
traits:
    - archetype
    - skill
archetype: Wandslinger
level: 4
prereq: Wandslinger Dedication, Expert in Crafting
---

When making Crafting checks to Craft or Repair wands, you can treat critical failures as failures.

**Special** If you chose wands for the Mark of Making Dedication, you double the circumstance bonus.
